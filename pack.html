1.1 About pack

 

The PACK Sanctuary is an organization located in Sanzhi district. Their job is to help stray animals, sick animals, heavily disabled animals, or animals that are hard to tame. They take care of dogs, cats, musk pigs, sheeps, a chicken. The Pack has two campuses, Cat Villa and Dog Kingdom. Cat Villa is a house in the mountains and the house has a roof to prevent the cat from escaping the house. Dog Kingdom located in a large land in a remote area in the mountains and there is no roof. They separate the animals using fences and group similar age, personality, or species together. The dogs, musk pigs, sheep, and chickens lives in the Dog Kingdom.

 

Sean is the founder of The Pack Sanctuary. Sean is  British and when he came to Taiwan and saw many stray animals on the street, and wanted to help them, so he created The PACK Sanctuary to help these stray and sick animals. For private reasons, he left The PACK Sanctuary in August 2017.

2.2 Job content

 

  • Provide an ideal life for the animals
    • feed the animals food on a daily basis
    • provide a clean environment
    • give the animals medicine if needed
    • check animals’ food, medication, and cleaning supplies
    • help animal admission process into the organization
    • record the animals’ daily behavior
    • record diet and medication habits
    • always update animal files
    • confirm the accuracy of the information
  • pay careful attention to the special needs of animals (disabled and aged animals)
  • cooperate with partners to help animals find their owners
    • provide more information about the animals to people
    • help the adopter understand the animal’s condition

2.3 purpose

The Pack has four main purposes. The first purpose is to not keep the animals in cage. Instead, keep them closest to a natural environment. They allow animals to run and jump in their natural surroundings rather than leaving them in a closed space. The second purpose is to prepare nutritional food everyday. Animals’ nutritious, fresh food: blends boneless raw chicken, vegetables, fish, oil, eggs, vinegar, garlic, and a small number of animal viscerals into vegetable juice. They provide each animal with food so they can grow up healthily, and their chances of getting sick can be reduced. The third purpose is never give up on any animal and provide food, space, and love for stray animals. The fourth purpose is never stop rescuing stray animals. They think they should save abandoned and wounded stray animals until they die. Another two purpose is they upholding the idea of not giving up on any animal and does not want any animals to be bought or sold
